Work Experience

Accelerate Mentor

University of Strathclyde

1 week in June 2023 & June 2024

Assist groups of highschool studients to engineer a product/service using technology for the betterment of people's lives. This was done using the SDLC whereby students must identify a group, target difficulties in their lives, conduct research/analysis, design and test a prototype for presentation by the end of the week.

In 2023, the group created Applify for newcomers to Glasgow city to consolidate city resources as online websites were often cluttered or hard to navigate. I am proud to mention that they managed to win first 1st place in the department.

In 2024, the group created an app for patients to sync medical devices to. This allows for live and accurate readings for doctors to diagnose as well as remotely monitor their patients health.

  • Assist students in groups to engineer a product/service using technology for the betterment of people's lives.
  • Explain concepts and offer challenges to their product to test its validity in the real world.
  • Help Challenge Leaders to organize materials and students during transport.
